About QuickPDFTools

QuickPDFTools was built with one simple idea: everyone deserves access to powerful PDF tools without paying a subscription or giving away their personal data.

Our Mission

PDF tools shouldn't be locked behind paywalls. We built 20+ essential tools that run entirely in your browser — meaning your documents never touch our servers. We never see your files. Period.

How It Works

Every tool on QuickPDFTools uses client-side JavaScript libraries like PDF-lib to process your documents locally. Your browser does all the work. There is no backend server receiving your files.

Why Free?

The site is supported by non-intrusive display advertising. Ads help cover development and hosting costs while keeping every tool 100% free for all users — forever.

Quick Facts

  • 20+ free PDF tools
  • Zero server uploads — 100% browser-based
  • No account or signup required
  • No file size limits
  • Works on all devices and browsers
  • Open and free forever
