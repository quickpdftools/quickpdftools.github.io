About This Tool

Our free PDF merger lets you combine any number of PDF files into a single document without uploading to any server. All merging happens locally in your browser using PDF-lib — a powerful JavaScript PDF library. This means your documents are 100% private and never seen by anyone else. Perfect for combining scanned documents, reports, invoices, or any PDFs you need joined together.
