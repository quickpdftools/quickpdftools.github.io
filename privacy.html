Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

QuickPDFTools ("we", "our", "us") operates the website at quickpdftools.github.io. This page explains our privacy practices. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

2. Files and Documents

We do not collect, store, or process your files on any server. All PDF processing happens entirely within your browser using JavaScript. Your documents never leave your device. We have zero access to the content of your files.

3. Cookies and Analytics

We may use Google Analytics to understand general site usage (pages visited, session duration, device type). This data is anonymized and does not include personally identifiable information. You can opt out via your browser settings or Google's opt-out tool.

4. Advertising (Google AdSense)

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings. Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on prior website visits.

5. Personal Data

We do not require you to create an account or provide personal information to use any of our tools. We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or payment information.

6. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us via our contact page.
